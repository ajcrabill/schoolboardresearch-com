We evaluate peer-reviewed research and credentialed policy reports on school board governance using the PEARLS framework. Suggest a study and we'll add it to the review queue.

What we review

  • Peer-reviewed journal articles
  • RAND, Brookings, IES policy reports
  • AASA, NSBA, McREL research
  • State education agency longitudinal studies
  • Meta-analyses and systematic reviews
  • Practitioner-facing research briefs with citations
